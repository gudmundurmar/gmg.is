Hi, I'm Guðmundur Már (or GMG)

If letters in your name aren't in the English alphabet... Does your name change? Or do you have two names?

Spelling

Like many other names the spelling changes when it leaves the country. It's written Gudmundur Mar in countries outside of Iceland since the Icelandic letter 'ð' (pronounced with a 'th' sound) is only available in the Icelandic alphabet. The same goes of for the á (pronounced 'aoo') even though it's available in a few more alphabets it might have a different pronuciation (so it's not worth the fuss keeping it there)

Pronunciation

The Icelandic pronunciation of the name is something like, Gvuth-muhn-duhr Mow-r, with rolling r's. Different spelling changes the pronunciation entirely so I've just introduced myself exactly like it's written outside of Iceland (Good-muhn-dur Mar). Whether the pronounciation is correct or not is really just based on my opinion since it's originally wrong anyway.

Handle

I use the handle gudmundurmar online when GMG is not available. Otherwise I'm quite fond of GMG since I've never really taken to a nickname. In Icelandic GMG is actually the equivalent of OMG in English.
